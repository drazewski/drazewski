About me

Hello! Welcome to my blog. My name is Lukasz Drazewski and I've been a frontend developer for several years.

Although I am geophysicist by education and I have spent over 10 years in the oil & gas industry, my last years of career I devoted to development as a web / frontend developer. I work with React on a daily basis and they hope to write the most on this blog. But not only. I enjoy working with UI and UX, I love CSS, I like e-commerce. I will try to write about all this on this blog.

Will this blog be technical only? Definitely not. Apart from coding, I have several other hobbies, but my greatest passion is just... reading and writing. I love searching the internet for interesting articles on various topics. I am very interested in history, especially the history of my country, Poland. I like visiting different corners of my country, learning about the history of less known places and facts. I hope to blog about it from time to time.

As my other passion is running, I really hope that from time to time I will post here short reports from my trainings or competitions.
